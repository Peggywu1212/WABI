WELCOME TO CASA WABI

Casa Wabi

Finds Beauty in Imperfection
and Depth of Nature

Casa Wabi is an art foundation established by Mexican painter and sculptor Bosco Sodi (@studioboscosodi) and built by Tadao Ando in 2014.Its purpose is to help artists engage with the local community, exchanging skills and knowledge as they create.For Casa Wabi, it meant building six sites in collaboration with eight leading architects, each of whom brought a particular approach to the place.

ADVENTURE

Above eye level captures the local and traditional spirit with the Palapa roofs. Whereas below eye level captures the contemporary essence with geometric concrete walls and the wooden shutters.

SELF-DISCOVERY

The Mexican architect Alberto Kalach designed the development of a 27 hectares garden, aiming to catalogue and preserve the surrounding species.

WORKSHOP

In this pavilion the clay workshops for children and young people are carried out conceptually connecting the usage of local clay with the apparent brick construction.
